What is |compass-short|?
------------------------

|compass| is the :abbr:`GUI (Graphical User Interface)` for MongoDB.
|compass-short| allows you to analyze and understand the contents of
your data without formal knowledge of MongoDB
:manual:`query syntax </tutorial/query-documents>`. In addition to
exploring your data in a visual environment, you can also use
|compass-short| to :ref:`optimize query performance <explain-plans>`,
:ref:`manage indexes <compass-indexes>`, and implement
:ref:`document validation <validation>`.

.. _compass-and-community-eds:

Compass Editions
----------------

Compass is available in several editions, with different
capabilities and limitations. See the :ref:`feature comparison table
<compass-feature-table>` for a comparison of the different editions.

Compass
~~~~~~~

Available to all MongoDB subscription users. Supports all features.

Compass Community Edition
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Available to all MongoDB users. Some Compass features are not
supported in the Community Edition.

.. _compass-readonly:

Compass Readonly Edition (*New in version 1.12*)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Available to all MongoDB subscription users. Users are limited to
**read operations** within MongoDB. This version of Compass does not
allow any create, update, or delete operations on documents,
collections, or indexes.

.. _compass-isolated:

Compass Isolated Edition (*New in version 1.14*)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Available to all MongoDB subscription users. Restricts network requests
to :abbr:`TLS (Transport Layer Security)`-encrypted
:abbr:`TCP (Transmission Control Protocol)` connections to the server
chosen on the :ref:`Connect <connect-run-compass>` screen. All other
outbound connections are not permitted in this edition.
